Definition Type: SimpleType
Name: FunctionalProperty
Containing Schema: hcm-sdt.xsd
Documentation: 
an enumeration of type xs:string which represents the denomination of the functional property or role of an structural member of the ship. The values are based on STEP Application
Protocol 218 (see ISO 10303-218, section 4.2.237.2).
