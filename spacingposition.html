Definition Type: ComplexType
Name: SpacingPosition
Containing Schema: hcm-sdt.xsd
Abstract
Documentation: 
A location on one of the global coordinate axes of the ship that is used as a reference point for any geometrical
or structural item during the design and manufacture of the ship (see ISO 10303-215, section 4.2.153).
