Name: MeasurementTask
Containing Schema: hcm-core.xsd
Abstract True
Documentation: 
Measurement tasks are used to break down the overall scope of measurements into manageable pieces. They are typically agreed between thickness measurement company, ship owner and class society during the planning meeting of a measurement campaign. Each measurement task defines areas on the hull structure that are to be measured or that were measured. These areas are specified by lists of structural parts, structural members or compartments. Additionally, a frame ranges may be given. Each measurement task contains an associated set of measurement points that shall be measured or that were measured.
